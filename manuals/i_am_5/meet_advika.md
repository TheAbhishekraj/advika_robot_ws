# Chapter 1: Meet Advika!

## What IS Advika?

Advika is a **real robot** that can:
- Drive around your room
- See things with two cameras (like eyes!)
- Sense how far away things are (like bat radar!)
- Talk to you with a robot voice
- Think and make decisions (with a little help)

Advika is about the size of a shoebox with wheels. She has:
- **Two big wheels** that make her go
- **A spinning laser** on top that maps the room
- **Two camera eyes** -- one looks forward, one looks at the floor
- **A tiny computer brain** (Raspberry Pi) inside
- **A battery** so she can move without a cord

## What Advika CAN Do

- Drive forward, backward, and spin in circles
- Find a red cone and stop in front of it
- Say "Hello!" and other words
- Avoid bumping into walls and furniture
- Follow you around like a puppy
- Play hide-and-seek (she tries to find you!)

## What Advika CANNOT Do (Yet!)

- Fly like a drone
- Pick up heavy things
- Go outside in the rain
- Climb stairs
- Make you a sandwich (sorry!)

## Advika's Personality

Advika is:
- **Curious** -- she loves exploring new rooms
- **Careful** -- she always checks for obstacles first
- **Polite** -- she says sorry if she bumps something
- **Brave** -- she tries new paths even when scared
- **Friendly** -- she wants to be your buddy!

## The Name "Advika"

In a language called **Sanskrit** (from a country called India), "Advika" means:
> "Unique. One of a kind. Unparalleled."

That means there is NOBODY else like Advika. Just like there is NOBODY else like YOU!
