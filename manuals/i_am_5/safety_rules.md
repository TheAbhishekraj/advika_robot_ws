# Chapter 2: Safety Rules -- Keeping Everyone Happy!

Robots are fun, but we need to be safe. These rules are **super important**.

## The Golden Rule

> **"If Advika is moving, a grown-up is watching."**

Never leave Advika running all by herself!

---

## The RED Button

Advika has a **big red button** on her back. This is the **EMERGENCY STOP**.

### When to press the red button:
- Advika is going somewhere she shouldn't
- A pet or baby is too close
- Something doesn't feel right
- You just want her to stop RIGHT NOW

### What happens when you press it:
- Advika stops ALL her wheels instantly
- She turns off her motors
- She can't move again until someone releases the button

**Practice pressing the red button!** It's fun and safe to practice.

---

## Kid Rules

1. **Ask first** -- Always ask a grown-up before turning Advika on
2. **Hands off wheels** -- Never touch the wheels while they're spinning
3. **Flat ground only** -- Advika only drives on floors, not tables or stairs
4. **No water** -- Keep drinks far away from Advika (water + electricity = bad!)
5. **Gentle touches** -- Be nice to Advika's cameras and laser
6. **Supervised play** -- A grown-up should be nearby when Advika is on

---

## Grown-Up Rules

1. **Test first** -- Run `test_peripherals.py` before each session
2. **Battery check** -- Never let the battery go below 9 volts
3. **Clear space** -- Remove small objects, cords, and fragile items from the play area
4. **E-Stop ready** -- Keep the emergency stop button within arm's reach
5. **Log review** -- Check `/var/log/advika/decisions.jsonl` after play sessions
6. **Firmware updates** -- Keep ESP32 firmware updated for safety patches

---

## The Traffic Light System

Advika has a little light that shows her "mood":

| Color | Meaning | What to Do |
|-------|---------|-----------|
| Green | Everything is good! | Play and have fun! |
| Blue | Advika is thinking | Wait a moment |
| Yellow | Advika sees something | Watch carefully |
| Red | STOP! Obstacle detected! | Check the area |
| White | Task complete! | Celebrate! |

---

## Safe Play Area Checklist

Before turning Advika on, check your room:

- [ ] Floor is clear of small toys and socks
- [ ] Cords are tucked away or taped down
- [ ] Stairs are blocked off
- [ ] Pets are in another room (or supervised)
- [ ] Baby siblings are not crawling nearby
- [ ] A grown-up is present and paying attention
- [ ] The red E-Stop button is easy to reach

---

## What If Something Goes Wrong?

| Problem | What to Do |
|---------|-----------|
| Advika won't stop | **PRESS THE RED BUTTON!** |
| Advika hits a wall | Check for damage, then restart |
| Smoke or smell | Turn off power immediately, tell a grown-up |
| Battery looks puffy | Do NOT use! Tell a grown-up right away |
| Advika is acting weird | Turn off, wait 10 seconds, turn back on |

---
