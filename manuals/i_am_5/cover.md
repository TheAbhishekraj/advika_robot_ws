# "I am 5" -- My Robot Friend Advika

**A guide for kids who want to play with, build, and be friends with a robot**

---

*Written for children ages 5 and up*
*With help from grown-ups who know about wires and code*

---

**Advika** means "one of a kind" -- and that's exactly what your robot is!
There is no other robot in the whole world exactly like yours.

**You are 5. Your robot is ready. Let's begin!**
